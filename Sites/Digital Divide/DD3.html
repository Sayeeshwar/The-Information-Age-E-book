Digital Divide

Because of where they live, the people there may not be exposed to computers. For example, people in lesser developed portions of Africa would not have even seen a computer probably. Did you know that only 7% of Africans are online? And that Africans constitute only 0.7 percentage of the internet users?

So, it is clear that this can be a major reason why there is a divide in several areas on a local scale. Technology penetration is not very great in developing countries. Also, how will people who are not educated operate computers?
