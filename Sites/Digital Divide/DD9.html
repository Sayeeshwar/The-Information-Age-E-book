Digital Divide

The pedal powered computer in Phon Kham

  1. These low power computers are powered by a pedal generator that can last for 10 years and are water proof.

  2. These have wireless PC cards that connect them to a hilltop relay station which is connected to a computer in a nearby town.

  3. These help in taking decisions for travel through the rugged and extreme terrain.
