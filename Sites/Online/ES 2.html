Online Service

Recently, learning that includes a virtual learning environment has grown popular. Students have an account where they can track their progress. Also, there is an on screen blackboard that teachers can operate with their computers.

Some of the disadvantages of online learning include not being present face to face for solving doubts. Also, since people can learn at their own pace, many tend to be lethargic and not committed compared to a classroom situation. Moreover, there are so many topics to choose from that it can be a bit overwhelming at times.
