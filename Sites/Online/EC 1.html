Online banking:

Going to the bank is a thing of the past. The power of the entire bank is accessible through computers nowadays. Paying bills, transferring money and seeing your bank accounts can all be done through the bank website or their app. Obviously, this facility needs to be secure to be safe against all cyber dangers such as hacking. There have been various attacks on bank databases primarily using phishing, pharming as well as malware and viruses such as Trojan horses. However, countermeasures have been taken to prevent such events. A security standard has been established and digital certificates have been given to various websites to show them as safe.

Although not many used it in the start, net banking is very famous nowadays. Almost all banks offer this facility.
