Social life

However, there is a better argument for how ICT has improved our social life. One major development is the creation of online dating websites and social networks. This has enabled a lot of people to meet their partners online. One example is the mobile app Tinder. This helps you to meet people in your locality of your choice.

You can then chat with them or meet in person. In my case, I managed to find some of my old friends on Facebook whom I had lost contact with. In the end, it is up to you whether you want to use it for the good or bad.

In conclusion, the effect of technology of social life is very profound. Some say it has a positive influence, most others say otherwise. A lot of time is wasted by people on social networks while it has enabled many people to meet new people. The overall aspect differs mainly from person to person
