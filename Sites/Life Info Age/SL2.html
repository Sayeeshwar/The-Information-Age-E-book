Social life

First off, when you can actually talk to someone in person, face to face, people send texts and video chat. This weakens the social relation between them and might lead to tension. Secondly, teenagers are so addicted to their smart phones and its content that they actually forget that there is a real world outside. According to the Kaiser Family Foundation, Americans between 8 and 18 spend one- third of their day with some sort of electronic device.
