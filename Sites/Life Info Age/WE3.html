Info Age

As the picture below illustrates, because of ICT, balance of work/life has been upset. And for work opportunities, although many have disappeared, many more have sprung up. One fantastic example is the call centre. Fifty years ago, there was nothing like them. Nowadays, they provide thousands of jobs to almost anybody who can speak good English.

To summarize, ICT has increased the efficiency and productivity in the office space. However, cost of new technology and old tech are huge blocks in the way of achieving that.
